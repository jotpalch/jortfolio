大學畢業典禮的時候買了一台 Fuji 即可拍
想說用底片拍拍看畢業的東西

拍了畢業典禮 家裡的狗 還有在新竹念書的高中同學在餐廳前面的合照
很多張沒有開閃光燈 室內的成像蠻差的

沖出來的時候才發現 很多張都暗暗糊糊的
但就是有一種數位拍不出來的感覺
可能是因為等了一段時間才看到 所以每張都會多看幾秒
那些拍壞的也捨不得丟

那是第一次覺得 拍照這件事情可以不只是記錄

後來就開始買底片機 換了不同的底片試
慢慢變成一個習慣

這篇算是這個網站的第一篇文章
想了一下要從什麼開始寫 覺得從這卷開始好了

---

Bought a Fuji disposable camera for my college graduation
Thought I'd try shooting film for the occasion

Shot the ceremony, my dog at home, and a group photo with high school friends studying in Hsinchu
Forgot to use the flash on most of them — the indoor shots turned out pretty bad

When I got them developed, most were dark and blurry
But there was something about them that digital couldn't replicate
Maybe it's because I waited days to see them — I ended up staring at each one a little longer
Even the bad ones I couldn't throw away

That was the first time I felt photography could be more than just documentation

After that I got a proper film camera and started trying different film stocks
It slowly became a habit

This is the first post on this site
Thought about where to start — figured I'd start with this roll
